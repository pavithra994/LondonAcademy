Who we are

The intense desire to share London Academy Sri Lanka was born in 2015 when its founder Ms. Amali Kottegoda who is a well experienced and qualified teacher, commenced preparing students for National Advanced Level and Ordinary Level examinations. With the tremendous success of the program, London Academy Sri Lanka was established as an academy providing National and International education introducing London Academy Sri Lanka was established as an academy providing National and international education introducing Cambridge and Edexcel OL @ AL curriculum. Introducing Cambridge & EDEXCEL OL & AL curriculum.

  • Mainly we are conducting group classes for National, Cambridge and EDEXCEL students above grade 6.

  • Also we are providing teachers for individual classes for National, Cambridge and EDEXCEL students above grade 6 who need special individual attention for their studies.

  • Working with the well qualified professional staff we have created a complete, interested teaching and learning experience to help you realize the full potential of your life, along with your choice.
